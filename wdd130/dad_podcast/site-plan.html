Content

Father's Podcast Home page

The people who listen to the podcast will come to this page looking for information about the podcast like who is the host what are some good episodes, guest who comes to the show and their quotes, they will learn in general what is the goal of the podcast they will read something like this “If you come here asking how to go through this family crisis where you don’t know how to react or what to do to help your sons and daughters this is the place to start” there will be links to the different platforms for listening the podcast like Spotify, Apple podcast, Stitcher and Goggle Podcast they will find some useful links to other pages like the list of episodes and the sign up for our Dad masterclass.

Images for Fathers Podcast home page

Episodes Page

Then people who is interested in listening more, will use this list page of episodes, with some highlights, interesting topics and guest, they will see the date of release and some others episodes that will be available soon with this format, the name of the episode, the topic or theme, like confidence, parenthood, new dad, marriage, then the date and who was the host and guess for the episode and some highlights of the episode or quote, with of course the link of the episode.

Images for the Episodes page

Sing Up Page

And finally, the podcast have developed a masterclass course with the help of guest and professionals like psychologists, for those who wants to learn more how to be a good father and because of the high demand and different time zones they need a way of contact more flexible, so a sing up page is the solution for them, this page will collect the name, address, country, phone number, email, and the name of the interested masterclass in the future this could be their account to register to other masterclass, and especial episodes, this is also a count to be a patreon and receive gifts, etc.
